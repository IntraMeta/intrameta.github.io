Web Hosting

Often providers want to offer a simple service for their customers to host content on the web, or make pictures and content available online for family and friends. IntraMeta offers a simple interface for providers to furnish additional services to their clients.

Users are able to login to their branded self-help and manage their files, directories and domain names.

Key Features:

  • Configurable storage and transfer limits
  • Offer tiered services with variable service levels
  • Support for user domain names
  • FTP access and configurable server settings for hosted files
